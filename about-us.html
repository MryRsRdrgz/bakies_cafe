About Us

A former OFW, who discovered his love for baking while working in a hotel in Macau and went on to establish his successful online cakeshop Bakiescafe, in the small island of Catanduanes in Virac.

A passionate and determined individual, embarked on a journey that would forever change his life. The path that would lead him to discover his true passion for baking.

Mission

Bakiescafe draws its mission to be a go-to bakeshop in Catanduanes that can satisfy each Catnadunganon's sweet cravings.

Vision

Bakiescafe envisions itself as a consistent front runner in the sweets and pastries, and events catering industries in the island where it could be known as having the best-tasting sweet offerings, products with highest value for money, and work ethics that is worthy of emulation.
